Learning and programming by yourself isn't much fun.  It's lame.

The Iron Yard is more than a content delivery system.  It is a collaboration.  It is a peer group going through the process together and helping one another along the way.  One's posse, one's crew.



I focus better and concentrate more in an environment where other people are doing the same thing.  That is a lesson I learned in the first week and promptly forgot.

(Okay, I did quit.  But only for like not even a whole day.  I missed everybody too much. )
