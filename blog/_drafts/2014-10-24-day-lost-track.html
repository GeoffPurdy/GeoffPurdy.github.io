Actually, Rails is more like an Enchanted Swamp.  You the developer carefully craft an object you think will please the mystical environment surrounding you and lift it up to the sky.  If your offering pleases the swamp gods, the landscape will snatch away your offering and reward you and you can advance.  If your offering is displeasing, the swamp gods reject it and you and your object sink down a little farther in the muck.
