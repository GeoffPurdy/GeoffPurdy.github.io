1:1 mentoring oversold

We started our first class with 7 students and grew it to a number that ensured that each and every person who comes through our program gets as much one-on-one mentorship time with the instructor as they need. In fact, that’s what our lab time is all about—not dozens and dozens of students trying to hack it out on their own with little or no access to the actual professor.
