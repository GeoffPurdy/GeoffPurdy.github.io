File under procrastination or a wave of creativity, but I felt compelled to create the meme image above.  This is one of my favorite lines from Sex and the City and it has been coming to mind a lot lately.  I attribute this to my stormy on-again off-again relationship with Rails, much like that between Carrie Bradshaw and Mr. Big.

I had a brief chat with Joe, one of the back-end devs at Smashing Boxes about some of my frustrations with Ruby on Rails and learned a few things.  Because of the loosely typed nature of Ruby, the IDE can't provide as much help as in a strongly typed language like Java.  The better Java IDE's can pretty much tell you what you want.  Ruby IDEs can do that, but to a lesser extent.
