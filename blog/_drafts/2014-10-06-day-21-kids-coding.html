Twenty eight to twelve year olds visited The Iron Yard for a three hour Kids Coding class.


First off the students broke into teams and wrote an algorithm to instruct a "robot" to make peanut butter and jelly sandwiches.

We can see the delicious results pictured.

Then the kids went on to demonstrate that they know Scratch better than any of the adults in the room.
