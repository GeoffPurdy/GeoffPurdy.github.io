Beginning makes the conditions perfect'' - Alan Cohen

I have intended to try blogging many times but have not.  Probably the biggest obstacle has been waiting for the perfect time and perfect ideas.  Finally, I decided to start anyway.

Primarily this will chronicle my experience as a student at The Iron Yard - a 13 week intensive code school with a time committment of 60-100 hours a week.  I will likely include other thoughts as well on papers and articles I find interesting.
