Geoff's First Coffeemandment:

1: He who emptieth the last cup must filleth the vessel.  For what good be the vessel lo it containeth no coffee?


This seems to be universal to the human condition. Everywhere I have ever worked, there is SOMEBODY who kicks the coffee at 9:30 and for whatever reason leaves it that way.
